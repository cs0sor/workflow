All of the tasks in the SugarCRM connector require a connection to SugarCRM, so you will need to create a connection with the type 'Sugar CRM'. SeeÂ [Connection Manager](connection-manager) for instructions on creating a new connection. You will see a screen like the one below, requesting your SugarCRM details:

![Sugar CRM Connection](http://www.zynk.com/images/v2/sugar_crm/sugar_crm_connection.png)

Enter your SugarCRM details as described below, then click 'Finish' to create the connection.

## Settings

### Password 
_Required_  
Enter the password for the SugarCRM user.

### URL 
_Required_  
Enter the address of your SugarCRM system. (e.g. http://crm.example.com).

### Username
_Required_  
Enter the username to log into SugarCRM with.
